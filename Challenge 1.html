The New Face of Education

            A world once bound by books alone,
            Now breathes through screens, in every home.
            Where knowledge flows like rivers wide,
            And technology stands as our guide.
        
 
            No longer just the pen and page,
            The classroom breaks from ancient cage.
            With code and clicks, the mind expands,
            New horizons shaped by our own hands.
        
    
            From distant lands, to distant skies,
            We witness truth with open eyes.
            A global pulse, a digital stream,
            Where every fact and every dream,
        
   
            Connects us all, from shore to shore,
            In ways we never knew before.
            To understand, to learn, to grow,
            Technology’s the key to know.
        
    
            The world evolves at rapid pace,
            And knowledge wears a digital face.
            For every child, for every soul,
            Let this be the shared, united goal.
        
            Embrace the screen, the code, the tool,
            For they shall build the future’s school.
            The new face of education bright,
            Leads us all into the light.
